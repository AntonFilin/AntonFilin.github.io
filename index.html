STARGAZERS VILLAGE 3.0

"THE GYPSIES CAMP"

For those who wanted but did not visit us last year

Settlement with unique forms of social and economic relations will appear in the Ural. Free accommodations, meals, tutoring are provided for anyone who wants. We only ask you to have strong desire to acquire farming skills and to be ready for changes in life.

What will we do?

In the beginning we will be divided into two groups. The first one will stay in the village taking care of the household. The second group will go into the field, apiary, and pasture. The group will start building a wood-house. A week later, the groups will exchange their work. At the end of first month a special working mode will be formed for each planter, based on his/her personal preferences.

In the course of year, we will grow vegetables; ranch fowls; build wood-houses, forge and bath-house for planters; do everything that is possible and necessary.

In the second year we will start cattle breeding and produce meat and dairy products for sale. At the same time we will got hides to dressing, and wool to spin. We are going to build bath-house and summer cattle-pen near the wooden-house. We will organize milking room, dig a well. Our aim is to be able to host 100 people at the same time in the end of second year. During the whole project we are open to everybody, so anyone can find us and come.

The camp is not for weak people.

It is not easy to survive on the ground. You will face with exhausting manual labor from sunrise till sunset, unusual living conditions, remoteness from civilization. And in the same time you will enjoy fresh air, healthy natural food, and friendly team. All of this will harden your body and your spirit. You will become a real planter.

You will acquire the leather working skills, woodcarving, metal forging, potting, plant-growing, livestock-breeding. You will study traditional crafts such as sleigh and harness manufacturing, weaving, braiding, knitting as well as hunting, fishing and horseback riding.

Free lunch

We, members of a social experiment Stargazers Village 2.0, invite you to participate in "The Gypsies Camp" not without a purpose. I'm going to demonstrate the principles of the sharing economy I had made and how it works for agriculture. For that I need people. Besides, one cannot fight single-handed. More people are better.

And besides, living here will be free only for those who will come "without a return ticket". If you want to visit us for a week or few, we would appreciate for any goods (as much as you want).

If you are already living in a village and planning to change the place, think about the opportunity to buy a house in here for 20,000 rubles, or land for 10,000 rubles in our neighborhood.

If you like our village, we will help you to get your own land and start farming.

From the author

I am hereditary farmer. I’ve grown up in a farm. In the best years my family had been holding horses, cows, pigs, sheep, goats, poultry, rabbits, bees. We used to cultivate the garden and the wheat field. I have a family estate, and 50 hectares of a land including fields and forests. There are also a vegetable garden, few greenhouses, dozen hives, mowing, household buildings, tools and equipment for a small company.

We are going to build new houses in accordance with the traditional technology using beams and moss and decorate the buildings with different wooden curls. We will use time-tested techniques and modern achievements.

I do not follow any religion or doctrine, do not confess vegetarianism. There will not be any rules or prohibitions for internal relationships in the Gypsies Camp the same as in the Stargazers Village 2.0. The pragmatic goals of the project are reviving agriculture in a particular village, demonstration of readiness of the society to transformation and new models, and helping to participants. We no longer call it experiment; we’ve already succeeded in the past year.

Why?

I answer as usual. Why not? The village is perfect for the project. There are little more than 50 yards. Only old people and just a few youth are living there, and they are happy with my idea. Several houses are empty, they can be repaired easily. Many plots of land are free; their owners do not want it for anything. During Soviet Union there was a big farm with about 10 thousand dairy cows, 1500 hectares of agriculture fields. They are empty now. The local government will never come up with anything better than my suggestion for the revival of that farming. A forester, a doctor, an elementary school teacher are needed there. Now there are no people to work, and no people to work for.

You can say there is a lot of work without payment. Yes that is true. Participants will have to work hard, but for the first year the amount of money will be hardly ever enough for food. We hope for financial assistance from crowdfunding. We are waiting for foreigners for cultural exchange, residence and education. Besides we don’t need much money - farming provides everything that is necessary for life. So initial investment are needed to provide rapid growth and high level of confidence for planters. For example, we need cows (couple), horses (couple), sheep (couple) and couple of chickens and other creatures, “two of every living thing of all flesh”. Financing is needed for internet line, without which the idea is meaningless. I have vegetables to grow on 0,4 hectare of land but nothing to eat especially if more than 10 people come. Don’t ask about hunger. Hunger in the farm is when you don’t look avoirdupois. Grab a bag of seeds, by the way, and you will never be hungry.

How?

This is not my first venture in the field of social research. Last year 12 people visited me. The year before, 60 people came to me (I didn’t count exactly).

Stargazers Village 1.0

I built a house, set up hot water, made beds and Internet connection. I invited friends and opened CouchSurfing. Guests were delighted with the place and ideas. And it inspired me for further work. I’d left this place for 6 months. Residents had to organize household themselves. They payed money for internet, electricity, and food. The experiment ended in complete failure. Participants turned this place into ruin and spoiled relationships with each other and even with the Thais. I kicked out everybody, including my wife. I had taught the lesson well and expressed it in the Users Agreement.

Stargazers Village 2.0

It has been organized in the same place, on the land of my Thai family. The villagers gave me two more houses and agreed to feed everyone who asks. Boomstarter project has been failed because I had to put myself crazy to save the little I had - my life. Family support was very helpful because I hadn’t had any money. I’d started to live alone and begun to wait for anyone who comes. Different people had arrived, 5 of them accepted the Users Agreement and stayed. Psychological work to eliminate problems in mutual relations was held with them. Conclusions from this work are formulated in Cyber-nihilist Declaration.

Cyber-nihilist Declaration will form the basis of Stargazers Village 3.0. There is no fanaticism, just drive. Many participants of the Stargazers Village 1.0 and 2.0 are still with me. It gives me hope that from the third time we will get something more than interesting memories. And we will place nontrivial plot twist into history books.

Why do I believe?

The fact that modern human being moves from a city to a village looks unnatural. Humanity has embarked on the path of consumption, which will lead the planet to a look of a huge metropolis with parks, greenhouses, fabrics ... Annual increasing of population in cities and degradation of thousands villages in recent years can prove that. Russia leads by these indicators. We are the children of services, and on a land children of nature are needed. We are children of corporations, but on a land nothing to manage. We love clothes, clubs, fashion ... we like to take, and the land should be given. But not everyone in this humanity has a place, and it gives me confidence at least someone will come to the Gypsies Camp.

Ancestors homelands are empty, people drink too much in villages, they die of old age or migrate to cities for work. They have pour education, pour initial conditions, and no friends to get good job in cities. But staying in an empty village looks like suicide.

Modern village communities are united on the basis of religion or teachings of a founder. Such communities become religious sects with strict rules even harder than in Orthodox monasteries. These adepts will be disappointed or embed into the new system, because the sects do not offer anything new. There are only zombie robots, with strictly regulated relationships. In such groups an authority grows into power, respect is used as a fakement, and everyone who disagree are going to be simply expelled.

The Cyber-nihilist Declaration describes social relationships without strife and force. It is all-round and all-sufficient. I invite you to come and check it out in practice.

The economic model hasn’t yet been completed for the global community, but it is enough for local demonstration.

Like last year, the participants will get a surprise, but for those who are ready and accept the declaration it will be positive
